About

My name is Aldo Ramirez, a 21-year-old with too many interests and not enough time. Despite this, I do my best to give them my attention, with church, school and my girlfriend Carsen as my first priorities.

I started illustrating in 2020 when I was a High School senior. I'm also a mediocre airsofter and a so-so guitarist. But that doesn't matter—as long as I'm enjoying myself!
